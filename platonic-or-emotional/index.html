Platonic or Emotional

March 9, 2022
Author: Tinjamin
Tags: [Life]

I’ve always struggled with finding the difference between a platonic relationship and an emotional one. To me, for some reason, the two always end up being intermingled and convoluted together. I hate it. Why is it that all of my close relationships turn into something that is emotional when I myself want to keep it platonic because I know that it won’t ever work out and the best thing to do is to keep it platonic. On top of this, I’m the type of person who wants to speak and say everything he feels; it often ends with the close relationship falling apart with many awkward tensions and emotions in between.

There are also certain elements that is disturbed by this confusion between platonic or emotional, an emptiness that prevails when you desire something more but at the same time know that such desire would turn your platonic relationship into something emotional. Perhaps it’s the conflicting desires that lead to such emptiness. Nothing is worse than spending time with someone close to you and leave feeling like you’ve drifted apart.
